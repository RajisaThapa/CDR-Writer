What is the average time it takes for a CDR to be delivered?

In most cases, assistance with the entire CDR preparation takes roughly three weeks.

To meet ACS criteria, how much job experience do I need to show?

The number of years of work experience you'll need is determined by your qualification level, ICT course content, and applicability of your qualification to the specified occupation. Only at the moment of formal assessment can this be determined.

Is it necessary for me to submit my academic project?

Yes, you can include academic work completed during your professional courses in your CDR as a Career Episode with supporting documentation.

What are the requirements for CDR report writing service? Do I need to submit any document?

Different credentials or documents which are important to assess your engineering degree through Competency Demonstration Report (CDR) are given are: One Passport Size Photograph, Bio Page of Identity Proof, CV / Resume, IELTS Result Card, Three Career Episodes, Certificate of Degree, Certified Academic Transcripts, 3 Career Episodes, Continual Professional Development (CPD) Report, Summary Statement
